Pan Cakes

Pancakes are flat, round cakes made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a leavening agent like baking powder. They are cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown and typically served with toppings such as syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a hearty, filling meal commonly enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It is especially popular in North India and is often served with accompaniments like pickles, yogurt, and a side salad of onions and green chilies. The combination of spicy, tangy chole with the soft, fluffy bhature makes it a beloved dish across India and among Indian cuisine enthusiasts worldwide.
